Explanation of Time Periods

Y.T. (Years of the Trees): This period refers to the time before the Sun and Moon were created, when the Two Trees of Valinor illuminated the world. It ends with the rising of the Sun and Moon.

F.A. (First Age): The First Age begins with the rising of the Sun and Moon and lasts until the defeat of Morgoth by the Valar at the end of the War of Wrath.

S.A. (Second Age): The Second Age follows the First Age and is marked by the rise of Númenor, the forging of the Rings of Power, and ends with the defeat of Sauron by the Last Alliance of Elves and Men.

T.A. (Third Age): The Third Age begins after the defeat of Sauron at the end of the Second Age and includes the events of "The Hobbit" and "The Lord of the Rings." It ends with the destruction of the One Ring.
